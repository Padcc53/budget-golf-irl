Welcome

Weather is such a big factor especially if you have shelled out to play a links course.


As a rule of thumb from October to early March the weather is in the lap of the Gods you could get anything.


From mid March to early October you can hope for the best but in Ireland with weather you always plan for the worst.


The best website to use is ireland's national weather forecasting service which is called Met Eireann and you can find it here


Remember that everyone plays in the same conditions, so no point in getting frustrated by the elements
